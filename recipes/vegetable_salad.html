Vegetable Salad

A vegetable salad recipe that is very easy to make, healthy and tastes very good!

This fresh summer vegetable salad is perfect for family lunch, dinner & during picnics.

Ingredients

  • 4 limes
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 large shallot, finely chopped (about 1/4 cup)
  • 360g thin green beans, trimmed
  • 2 ears fresh corn, kernels removed (about 1 1/2 cups kernels)
  • 1 red capsicum, seeded, diced
  • 2 Lebanese cucumbers, quartered lengthways, thinly sliced crossways into wedges
  • 350g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 3 spring onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 fresh long red chilli, very thinly sliced into roundss
  • 1/3 cup fresh coriander leaves, coarsely chopped
  • 1 avocado, peeled, pitted, diced

Steps to prepare

  1. Take a bowl.
  2. Cut vegetables and fruits into small slices and place in the bowl.
  3. Add virgin olive oil and mix well.
  4. Squeeze lemon onto the vegetables and fruits.
  5. Mix well with a spoon.
  6. Place the salad dish to marinate for 5 minutes.
  7. Ready to serve and eat.
